Gauge type refers to the distance between the inner sides of the two rails on a railway track. It determines which trains can run on a line. In India, gauge types is important because the shift from metre and narrow gauges to broad gauge reflects the country's modernisation and standardisation of its rail network, improving connectivity and efficiency.
